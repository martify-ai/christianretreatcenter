Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bookings

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for individual or small group retreats, and 1-3 months ahead for larger groups or events. For peak seasons (December-January, Easter period, and August), booking 3-6 months in advance is advisable to secure your preferred dates.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Full refund for cancellations made 30 or more days before arrival
  • 75% refund for cancellations 15-29 days before arrival
  • 50% refund for cancellations 7-14 days before arrival
  • No refund for cancellations less than 7 days before arrival

Special circumstances may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Can I visit before booking for a large group?

Yes, we encourage site visits for group organizers. Please contact us to arrange a convenient time to tour our facilities. This helps you better understand the space and plan accordingly for your group's needs.

Do you provide bedding and towels?

Yes, all accommodation options include bedding (sheets, pillows, blankets). Towels are provided for guests in private rooms and family suites. For dormitory accommodations, we recommend bringing your own towels, though a limited supply is available upon request.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergies with advance notice. Please specify any special dietary requirements in your booking form at least one week before arrival. An additional charge of 10% may apply for specialized meal preparations.

Terms and Conditions

1. Booking and Confirmation

All bookings require a 50% deposit to be confirmed. Bookings are not guaranteed until deposit payment is received and confirmation is sent by the Christian Retreat Centre.

2. Payment Terms

Final payment is due 7 days prior to arrival. Failure to make final payment may result in cancellation of the booking without refund of deposit.

3. Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Full refund for cancellations made 30 or more days before arrival
  • 75% refund for cancellations 15-29 days before arrival
  • 50% refund for cancellations 7-14 days before arrival
  • No refund for cancellations less than 7 days before arrival

4. Facility Care and Damage

Guests are responsible for treating the facilities with respect. Any damage caused by guests will be charged to the booking party. A security deposit may be required for certain group bookings.

5. Code of Conduct

As a Christian retreat centre, we expect all guests to respect the spiritual nature of our facility. This includes:

  • No alcohol or illegal substances on the property
  • No smoking inside any buildings (designated outdoor areas only)
  • Respecting quiet hours (10:00 PM - 7:00 AM)
  • Appropriate dress and conduct

6. Liability

The Christian Retreat Centre is not responsible for loss, theft, or damage to personal belongings. Guests are advised to take appropriate precautions.

7. Privacy Policy

Personal information collected during the booking process is used solely for retreat administration purposes and will not be shared with third parties except as required by law.

8. Changes to Bookings

Any changes to bookings must be requested in writing and are subject to availability. Significant changes may be treated as a cancellation and re-booking.

9. Force Majeure

The retreat centre reserves the right to cancel bookings due to unforeseen circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, facility issues, etc.). In such cases, full refunds will be provided or alternative dates offered.
