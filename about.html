About Us

Our journey, mission, and vision

Our Story

The journey of Christian Retreat Centre

Founded in 2005, the Christian Retreat Centre began with a vision to create a sanctuary where people could step away from the noise and distractions of everyday life to connect with God and experience spiritual renewal.

What started as a small property with limited facilities has grown into a comprehensive retreat center serving thousands of visitors each year. Through faithful stewardship and God's blessing, we've expanded our grounds and services while maintaining the peaceful atmosphere that makes our retreat center special.

Over the years, we've been privileged to host countless individual retreats, church gatherings, conferences, and spiritual formation events that have impacted many lives. Our journey continues as we seek to provide an environment where people can experience God's presence and transformative power.

Our Mission

To provide a peaceful, nurturing environment where individuals and groups can disconnect from distractions, deepen their relationship with God, and experience spiritual renewal through prayer, reflection, and community.

Our Vision

To be a premier Christian retreat destination known for creating transformative spiritual experiences in an atmosphere of peace, hospitality, and excellence that inspires visitors to continue their spiritual growth long after they leave our grounds.
