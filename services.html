Our Services

Supporting your retreat journey from start to finish

At Christian Retreat Centre, we offer comprehensive services designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals and groups seeking spiritual renewal, community building, and meaningful gatherings. Our experienced staff is dedicated to ensuring your retreat experience is seamless and impactful.

Our services are customizable to accommodate different group sizes, retreat purposes, and budget considerations. We invite you to explore our offerings and contact us for personalized planning assistance.

Booking Information

Important details to know before you book

Reservation Policy

  • 50% deposit required to confirm bookings
  • Full payment due 7 days before arrival
  • Bookings should be made at least 2 weeks in advance
  • Peak seasons require 1 month advance booking

Cancellation Policy

  • Full refund for cancellations 30+ days before
  • 75% refund for cancellations 15-29 days before
  • 50% refund for cancellations 7-14 days before
  • No refund for cancellations less than 7 days before

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in: 2:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Check-out: By 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in or late check-out available upon request (additional fees may apply)

Payment Methods

  • Bank transfer
  • Online payment (cards accepted)
  • Mobile money
  • Cash (for balance payments only)
