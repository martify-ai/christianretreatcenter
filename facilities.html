Facilities Overview

Everything you need for a meaningful retreat experience

Our Christian Retreat Centre is designed to provide a serene and comfortable environment for spiritual growth, reflection, and community gatherings. Our facilities combine natural beauty with practical amenities to ensure your retreat experience is both meaningful and comfortable.

Whether you're planning an individual retreat, a church group event, or a large conference, our diverse facilities can accommodate your needs while maintaining the peaceful atmosphere essential for spiritual connection.

Accommodation

Comfortable lodging for restful retreats

Retreat Rooms and Dormitories

Our accommodation options include:

  • Private Rooms: 20 comfortable rooms with en-suite bathrooms, perfect for individual retreatants or couples seeking privacy.
  • Family Suites: 5 spacious suites that can accommodate families with children or small groups.
  • Dormitories: 4 dormitory-style rooms that can house up to 15 people each, ideal for youth groups or large gatherings.

All accommodation options include comfortable beds, clean linens, adequate ventilation, and basic amenities to ensure a restful stay.

Prayer & Reflection Spaces

Sacred spaces designed for spiritual connection

Dedicated Areas for Prayer and Meditation

Our retreat centre features several spaces specifically designed for prayer, reflection, and connecting with God:

  • Prayer Garden: A beautifully landscaped outdoor area with quiet nooks, benches, and scriptural markers to guide personal meditation.
  • Prayer Chapel: An intimate indoor chapel that accommodates 30 people for quiet worship, available 24/7 for personal prayer.
  • Meditation Walk: A winding path through natural surroundings with stations for reflection and scripture reading.
  • Lakeside Prayer Points: Seating areas by our small lake, offering a peaceful setting for contemplation and prayer.

These spaces are maintained as quiet zones to ensure they remain conducive to spiritual reflection and connection.

Dining Facilities

Nourishing meals in a communal setting

Dining Hall & Kitchen

Our retreat centre provides excellent dining facilities to ensure your physical nourishment complements your spiritual journey:

  • Spacious Dining Hall: Communal dining area that seats up to 200 people.
  • Full-Service Kitchen: Modern kitchen facilities where our staff prepare fresh, nutritious meals.
  • Special Diets: Ability to accommodate various dietary requirements with advance notice.
  • Meal Options: Full board, half board, or self-catering options available for groups.

Mealtimes at the retreat centre provide not only physical nourishment but also opportunities for fellowship and community building among retreat participants.
