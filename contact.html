Our location and directions

Directions

From Lagos Island

  1. Take the Third Mainland Bridge towards Ikorodu
  2. Continue on Lagos-Ikorodu Road for approximately 25km
  3. At Ikorodu roundabout, take the second exit towards Ago-Iwoye
  4. Continue for 5km, then turn right at the Christian Retreat Centre signpost
  5. Follow the access road for 1km to reach the centre entrance

Estimated travel time: 1 hour 30 minutes (depending on traffic)

From Lagos Mainland

  1. Take Lagos-Ikorodu Road heading towards Ikorodu
  2. Continue for approximately 20km until you reach Ikorodu town
  3. At Ikorodu roundabout, take the second exit towards Ago-Iwoye
  4. Continue for 5km, then turn right at the Christian Retreat Centre signpost
  5. Follow the access road for 1km to reach the centre entrance

Estimated travel time: 1 hour (depending on traffic)

Frequently Asked Contact Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during our office hours (Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM). For urgent matters, we recommend calling our main office directly.

Can I schedule a site visit before booking?

Absolutely! We encourage potential guests, especially those planning group events, to visit our retreat centre before booking. Please contact us to arrange a convenient time for your visit.

Is there public transportation available to the retreat centre?

Public transportation options are limited. The nearest bus stop is about 1km from our entrance. For groups, we recommend arranging private transportation or inquiring about our shuttle service from key locations in Lagos.

Who should I contact for specific inquiries about hosting an event?

For event hosting inquiries, please contact our Events Coordinator directly at events@christianretreatcentre.com or call our booking line at +234 987 654 3210. They can provide detailed information about our facilities, availability, and customization options for your event.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or make a booking, we collect personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and address
  • Booking details and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment providers)
  • Communications with our staff

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for:

  • Processing bookings and providing our services
  • Communicating with you about your inquiries or bookings
  • Improving our services and facilities
  • Sending relevant updates about our retreat centre (if you've opted in)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When necessary for providing our services (e.g., payment processors)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Rectify inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to our legal obligations)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@christianretreatcentre.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or email.
