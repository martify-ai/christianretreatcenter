Welcome to Our Retreat

A haven of peace for spiritual growth and reflection

Our Christian Retreat Centre is a sanctuary designed for those seeking spiritual renewal, peaceful reflection, and connection with God. Located in the serene environment of Ago-Iwoye, Ikorodu, we offer a perfect escape from the noise and distractions of everyday life.

Whether you're planning a personal retreat, a church gathering, or a spiritual conference, our facilities provide the ideal setting for meaningful experiences and divine encounters.
